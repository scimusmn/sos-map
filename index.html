Pat’s
Saint Paul Penchants, Preferences and Predilections

Welcome! I have lived in both of the Twin Cities over the past 34 years, but Saint Paul now is my hometown since my wife and I bought a house in the city over 20 years ago. Saint Paul has many eclectic charms. I hope that you find time to enjoy some of my favorite sites. It can be challenging to roam far when attending a conference, but I have included some stretch destinations just beyond downtown if you are willing to go a bit out of your way.
